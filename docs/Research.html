My background research involves how visual aestheitics and colour preference elicit emotional responses through judegment. That is, different colours and textures of art objects affect judgements and thus produce an emotional response.Currently, in my MSc Research Methods of Psychological Science master’s I am investigating material perception of liquids and whether certain liquids with different colours and viscosities elicit an emotional response and does this effect the judgements of observing different visual aesthetics.

To further my studies, i would be willing to include different factors that may alter judgements in the field of visual aesthetics and develop analsysis further in colour preference and liquids. I am throughly interested in this field as i paint, install art works in exhibitions and in general work with art objects, and through a psychological perspective i find it interesting to connect both fields together and how the two factors connect.
